Our Work

Empowering Nurses with AutoSchedule

At AutoSchedule, we understand the challenges nurses face in managing their busy schedules. That's why we created AutoSchedule, a mobile app designed to streamline the process and give nurses back control of their time.

Streamlined Scheduling:

  • Effortless Availability Management: Easily input your availability preferences, including desired hours, preferred days off, and blackout dates, all within a user-friendly interface.
  • Shift Visibility at Your Fingertips: Browse open shifts posted by managers, with clear details like date, time, and unit. No more scrambling for the latest schedule updates.
  • Seamless Shift Claiming: Claim shifts that fit your needs with a single tap. Reduce the time spent on back-and-forth communication or missed opportunities.
  • Improved Communication: Communicate directly with managers regarding shift changes or questions through a built-in chat functionality. Foster a more collaborative scheduling environment.
