About Us

Welcome to AutoSchedule, your go-to solution for managing nursing schedules with ease and efficiency. Our platform empowers nurses to take control of their work-life balance by effortlessly managing availability, browsing, and claiming shifts that fit their needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the way nursing schedules are managed by providing a seamless, user-friendly platform that enhances productivity and improves the work-life balance for healthcare professionals.

Our Vision

We envision a world where nurses can focus on providing excellent patient care without the stress of managing complex schedules. AutoSchedule aims to be the leading solution in workforce management for the healthcare industry.
